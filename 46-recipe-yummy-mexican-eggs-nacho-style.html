Ingredients of Mexican eggs nacho style

  1. You need 2 tbsp of olive oil.
  2. It's 1 of onion, chopped.
  3. Prepare 2 of cloves garlic crushed.
  4. Prepare 1 tsp of smoked paprika, cumin and dried oregano.
  5. It's 1/4 tsp of cinnamon.
  6. You need 1 of 400gm tin crushed tomatoes.
  7. It's 1/4 cup of water or stock.
  8. Prepare 1 of leftover chilli con carne mix.
  9. It's 1 of chili powder to taste.
  10. You need 1 of salt and pepper to taste.
  11. Prepare 8 of eggs.
  12. Prepare 1 of bag of corn chips any flavor.
  13. You need 1/2 cup of grated cheese.
  14. You need 1 of advacado sliced and sour cream to serve.

Mexican eggs nacho style step by step

  1. Pre heat oven 180degress celsius.Heat oil in fry pan cook onion and garlic till soft..
  2. Add spices and cook 1min then add chilli con carne, tomatoes and water and chilli powder bring to boil then simmer 10mins.
  3. Pull pan off heat make holes in the sauce and crack the eggs into them,season eggs and scatter with grated cheese,arrange corn chips around the edge of pan,.
  4. Bake in oven 10-12 mins until eggs are set pull out of oven..
  5. Top with sliced advacado, and sour cream and serve.
