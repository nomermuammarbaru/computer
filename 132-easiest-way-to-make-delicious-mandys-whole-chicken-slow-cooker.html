Ingredients of Mandys whole chicken slow cooker

  1. You need 4 lb of whole fresh chicken.
  2. It's 3 of carrots cut into chunks.
  3. You need 1 of red bell pepper.
  4. You need 2 of onion, chopped.
  5. It's 2 lb of red potato cut into chunks.
  6. Prepare 1 tsp of chili flakes.
  7. Prepare 1 tsp of garlic powder.
  8. You need 1 tsp of onion powder.
  9. You need 1 tbsp of smoked paprika.
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp of dried parsley.
  11. You need 1 tbsp of dried thyme.
  12. It's of salt and pepper.
  13. You need 200 ml of cold water (optional).
  14. You need 2 tbsp of cornstarch (optional).
  15. It's of cold water (optional).

Mandys whole chicken slow cooker step by step

  1. Cut up veg into chunks place in bottom of the slow cooker..
  2. Add whole chicken and all the seasonings and the water, cover with a lid and cook on high for 6 hours, and serve enjoy.
  3. (Optional) When fully cooked remove carefully and slice, if u want to thicken the liquid use 2 tbsp of cornstarch and a little water add to boiling liquid and thicken the stock/liquid to a gravy yum.
