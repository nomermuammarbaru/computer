Vickys Garlic & Herb Chicken and Mushrooms, GF DF EF SF NF step by step

  1. Preheat the oven to gas 5 / 190C / 375°F and spray an ovenproof dish lightly with oil.
  2. Put the chicken in the dish and season with a little salt & pepper.
  3. Sprinkle the mushrooms with the lemon juice, then add them to the dish with the garlic butter slice https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/357071-vickys-savoury-compound-butters-gluten-dairy-egg-soy-free.
  4. Give the dish another spray of oil then bake, uncovered for 25 minutes, basting with the juices halfway through.
  5. Serve with seasonal vegetables.
