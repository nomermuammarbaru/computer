'fish and chips' from bbq instructions

  1. Score the sides of the fish. This will help cooking thru. Season the fish and leave in the fridge for an hour..
  2. Cut the carrots into a shape of skinny fries. Toss it in with cornflour and cayenne pepper. Add 1 tbsp of olive oil and mix it in..
  3. On a roasting tray set the carrot fries and place on bbq avoiding direct heat. Cook it until they get crispy outside and soft inside..
  4. To make a glaze, mix orange juice, soy sauce and honey. Glaze the fish with the brush from both sides and then place it into metal fish rack and set on bbq over direct heat.
