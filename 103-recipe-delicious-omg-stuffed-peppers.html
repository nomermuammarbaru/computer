OMG Stuffed Peppers step by step

  1. Switch grill on to a medium setting.
  2. Slice red peppers in half and start grilling them, open cavity down (outer flesh sides up).
  3. Meanwhile, chop yellow peppers and pork up small. Slice sundried tomatoes in half, add some garlic, coriander and paprika if you like.
  4. Shallow fry the above in a pan until cooked!.
  5. Slice the goat's cheese into smallish slices.
  6. Turn red peppers over and spoon the mixture above into each one. Top with slices of goat's cheese and return to the grill for 12 mins or so, or until sufficiently cooked.
  7. Sprinkle with coriander to serve.
