Ingredients of Vickys Winter Pork, Red Cabbage and Cranberries, GF DF EF SF NF

  1. Prepare 1500 grams of diced pork.
  2. You need 1 tbsp of dried thyme.
  3. It's of salt & pepper.
  4. It's 3 tbsp of oil, divided.
  5. It's 2 of onions, sliced.
  6. It's 1000 grams of red cabbage, shredded.
  7. Prepare 2 of red apples, peeled, cored and sliced.
  8. It's 425 ml of red wine.
  9. Prepare 1 of cinnamon stick.
  10. Prepare 200 grams of chestnuts (optional).
  11. Prepare 3 tbsp of cranberry sauce.

Vickys Winter Pork, Red Cabbage and Cranberries, GF DF EF SF NF step by step

  1. Preheat the oven to gas 3 / 160C / 325Â°F.
  2. Toss the pork in the thyme and season with a sprinkle of salt and pepper. Set aside.
  3. Heat 2 tablespoons of the oil in a large casserole dish on the stove top over a medium heat and fry off the onions until softened.
  4. Stir in the cabbage, apples and wine and cook until the cabbage starts to wilt.
  5. Add the chestnuts if using, 2 tablespoons of the cranberry sauce and the cinnamon stick and bring to the boil. Cover and turn down to a simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Meanwhile heat the remaining oil in a frying pan and brown off the pork.
  7. Stir the last tablespoon of cranberry sauce into the pork then add to the casserole dish, stirring well.
  8. Put the lid on the casserole dish and put in the oven for 90 minutes until the pork is tender.
