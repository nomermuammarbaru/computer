  9. Prepare 250 ml of beef stock.
  10. You need 1 tbsp of margarine.

Another cottage pie step by step

  1. Boil the potatoes until soft to mash..
  2. Brown mince with freshly ground black pepper and reserve.
  3. Sautee the chopped onion until soft and add the carrots finely chopped..
  4. Add the worcestershire sauce, breadcrumbs, nutmeg and stock. Let it simmer. Add meat..
  5. Place mince mixture in a dish suitable for the oven..
  6. Mash the potato with a tablespoonful of margerine. Add a beaten egg and place on top of the meat.
  7. Cook in a pre heated oven at 200C for 30 minutes.
