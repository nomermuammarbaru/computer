Ingredients of Balti Gosht

  1. It's 500 gms of mutton with bones.
  2. You need 3 tbsp of oil.
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp of ghee.
  4. Prepare 2 of onion, chopped.
  5. It's 1 tbsp of ginger-garlic paste.
  6. It's 1 tbsp of coriander powder.
  7. Prepare 1 tsp of cumin powder.
  8. It's 1 tbsp of red chilli powder.
  9. Prepare 1/2 tsp of pepper powder.
  10. You need 1 tsp of garam masala powder.
  11. It's 1 of tomato, chopped.
  12. You need to taste of salt.
  13. Prepare 1/2 cup of yoghurt.
  14. It's 2-3 of green chilies.
  15. Prepare 1 tsp of ginger julienne.
  16. You need 1 tsp of coriander leaves.

Balti Gosht step by step

  1. Heat 2 tbsp oil in a pan and saute the onion till light brown in colour. Drain and keep aside..
  2. Heat remaining oil and ghee. Saute the mutton for 4-5 minutes or till they are well browned..
  3. Add all the dry spices (except salt) mixed with some water. Mix well and stir fry till the oil separates..
  4. Now add the tomatoes and salt. Saute for 2 minutes..
  5. Add 1/2 cup water and the fried onion. Pressure cook for 15-20 minutes on a low flame after the first whistle..
  6. Transfer the cooked mutton back to the pan. Add the yoghurt and green chilies. Simmer till the gravy reduces to the desired consistency and the oil separates..
  7. Garnish with ginger julienne & coriander leaves. Serve with plain biryani, pulao, naan, kulcha or tandoori roti..
