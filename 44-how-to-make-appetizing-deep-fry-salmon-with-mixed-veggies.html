Deep fry salmon with mixed veggies instructions

  1. Deep fry salmon>.
  2. Put the pan on the stove in middle high heat, add rice bran oil and wait until hot.
  3. Put salmon into the pan and deep fry until golden brown or cooked. Then remove from pan to a serving plate..
  4. Veggies>.
  5. Clean veggies and cut all veggies based on your choice, put into a bowl (suitable for microwaving), add water to just cover the veggies..
  6. Put in the microwave for around 4-5 minutes (My microwave @1200W within 5 minutes) or until cooked..
  7. Remove all veggies from the bowl and put into cold water for around 1 minute, then remove from the water into a serving dish..
  8. Serve deep fried salmon with mixed veggies and sprinkle with salt and pepper. Enjoy :).
